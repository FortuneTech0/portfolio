About Me

Hello! My name is Benson Fortune Chimuanya, a final-year Computer Science student at Institut Universitaire La Grâce (IUG), Republic of Benin. I am a passionate frontend developer and aspiring full-stack engineer with a deep interest in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly web applications.

My journey into technology began with HTML and CSS, creating simple static pages that grew into full responsive designs. Over time, I expanded my knowledge into JavaScript and frameworks, version control with Git/GitHub, and continuous improvement in UI/UX principles. My ambition is to contribute to innovative projects and grow into a professional software developer capable of delivering impactful solutions.
